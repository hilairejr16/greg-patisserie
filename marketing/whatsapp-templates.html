💡 Tips for Best Results · Conseils pour de meilleurs résultats

  • Always personalize with the client's first name — it dramatically increases response rates
  • Send on Tuesday–Thursday between 10am–12pm or 6pm–8pm for best open rates
  • Add a seasonal twist: mention Mother's Day, Christmas, or upcoming holidays
  • For existing clients, attach a photo of a recent creation to make it visual
  • Follow up once after 3 days if no response — keep it brief and friendly
  • For corporate clients, call first, then follow up with this WhatsApp message
