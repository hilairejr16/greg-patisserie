Notre Histoire · Our Story

Gregory Olivier

✦

At the heart of Greg's Patisserie is Gregory Olivier — a chemist by profession and a passionate artisan in the world of culinary arts and baking. Blending precision with creativity, Gregory founded Greg's Patisserie to share the rich flavors of Caribbean cuisine, with a special tribute to his Haitian roots, serving communities across Canada.

For Gregory, baking and cooking are far more than skills — they are expressions of love, culture, and connection. Every dish and every pastry is crafted with intention, telling a story of heritage while bringing people together. Through his creations, he offers not just food, but an experience — one that reconnects clients to tradition and introduces them to the vibrant essence of Caribbean flavor.

With years of experience behind him, Gregory continues to pour his heart into every recipe, sharing his passion and honoring his culture — one bite at a time.
