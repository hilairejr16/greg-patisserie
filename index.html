Notre Histoire

Greg Patisserie

✦

Founded by Gregory Olivier in Laval, Québec, Greg Patisserie was born from a passion for artisan baking and a desire to share the rich flavors of Caribbean and French-Canadian traditions.

Every creation is handcrafted with premium ingredients, honoring both the Haitian community's vibrant culture and the Quebec tradition of fine pastry-making.

From intimate birthday cakes to grand wedding receptions, Greg Patisserie brings sweetness to life's most important moments.
